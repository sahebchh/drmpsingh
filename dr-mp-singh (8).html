About the Doctor

Decades of Dedicated
Neurological Care

Dr. M.P. Singh is the Director of Neurosciences at Lotus Super Speciality Hospital, Agra, and one of the most decorated neurologists in the region. He holds an MD in Medicine and a DM in Neurology — the highest postgraduate neurology qualification in India.

A gold medallist and nationally recognised academic, Dr. Singh has served as Vice President of the Indian Association of Clinical Medicine (IACM) at the national level, and as Convenor of its National Online CMEs. He has authored more than 50 publications in peer-reviewed national journals.

His areas of clinical interest are Headache, Stroke, and Epilepsy. Known for his empathetic approach and diagnostic precision, Dr. Singh is one of the most trusted neurological specialists in Agra, serving patients from across Uttar Pradesh and beyond.
