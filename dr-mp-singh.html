About the Doctor

Decades of Dedicated
Neurological Care

Dr. M.P. Singh is a distinguished Neurologist and Neurophysician based in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, holding the highest postgraduate neurology qualification in India — a DM (Doctorate of Medicine) in Neurology, along with an MD in General Medicine and an MBBS.

With over 10 years of specialised neurological practice across multiple cities in India, Dr. Singh has contributed to research, participated in national neurology workshops, and published papers in the field. He is a recipient of the Lifetime Achievement Award for his contributions to neurology in the region.

Known for his empathetic, patient-centred approach and diagnostic precision, Dr. Singh is one of the most trusted neurological specialists in Agra, serving patients from across Uttar Pradesh and beyond.
